# Downloaded Papers

## Core Papers (Deep-Read)

1. **Localizing Persona Representations in LLMs** (2505.24539_localizing_persona_representations.pdf)
   - Authors: Cintas, Rateike, Miehling, Daly, Speakman (IBM Research)
   - Year: 2025
   - arXiv: 2505.24539
   - Why relevant: PCA on persona activations across layers; Deep Scan identifies persona-specific activation subsets; maps geometric overlap between 14 personas

2. **Persona Vectors: Monitoring and Controlling Character Traits in Language Models** (2507.21509_persona_vectors_monitoring_controlling.pdf)
   - Authors: Chen, Arditi, Sleight, Evans, Lindsey
   - Year: 2025
   - arXiv: 2507.21509
   - Why relevant: Automated persona vector extraction pipeline; SAE decomposition of persona vectors into interpretable features; raises "does a natural persona basis exist?" as open question

3. **PERSONA: Dynamic and Compositional Inference-Time Personality Control** (2602.15669_persona_compositional_activation_vector_algebra.pdf)
   - Authors: Feng et al.
   - Year: 2026 (ICLR)
   - arXiv: 2602.15669
   - Why relevant: Most directly relevant — validates approximately orthogonal Big Five vectors supporting vector algebra; 8.8:1 causal orthogonality ratio

4. **The Geometry of Persona** (2512.07092_geometry_of_persona.pdf)
   - Authors: Wang
   - Year: 2025
   - arXiv: 2512.07092
   - Why relevant: Forces orthogonality via W^T W = I loss; personality orthogonal to reasoning circuits

5. **Representation Engineering** (2310.01405_representation_engineering.pdf)
   - Authors: Zou, Phan, Chen, Campbell, et al.
   - Year: 2023 (updated 2025)
   - arXiv: 2310.01405
   - Why relevant: Foundational methodology — LAT with PCA for reading/controlling concept directions

6. **Rediscovering the Latent Dimensions of Personality with LLMs** (2409.09905_latent_dimensions_personality_llm.pdf)
   - Authors: Suh et al.
   - Year: 2024 (NeurIPS)
   - arXiv: 2409.09905
   - Why relevant: SVD on log-probabilities recovers Big Five as top-5 principal components (74.3% variance)
